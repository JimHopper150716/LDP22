The Theme

The Easter Egg Hunt

Theme of this Cryptic Hunt

Hello, hello, hello players.

Well, this year's hunt is a full-blown treasure hunt! Yeah like those Dan Brown Novels and the Nicholas Nage movies. No seperate questions. Just one long hunt. Let me tell you the gist of it. You will be provided with a goal, that is, to find my hidden easter egg, which is your prize. Wait, don't worry, you will still be awarded with the cash prizes, the Egg represents that prize. You will begin at the starting gate, and you'll go on an online adventure around the Internet, decoding ciphers and codes and moving ahead until you find the treasure, My Easter Egg. The Hunt is divided into 3 levels. At the end of each level you will obtain a key to get you through to the next level, the person who obtains all the 3 keys will be automatically transported to the treasure. Sounds pretty easy right? Hmm, who am I to say otherwise. So, in a nutshell, it is a Treasure Hunt. But, what's the fun in that! So, for your pleasure, or torment, the hunt is in the style of Cicada 3301. Anything is interconnected to everything, or nothing. Wait, I'm confusing myself. Anyway, so a hunt, one thing leads you to another, until you reach the Easter Egg. No answer is straight away that easy, you will have to work hard in order to figure out your way around it. You'll understand this in due time.

We'll take some precautions.

There's gonna be a couple ground rules.
